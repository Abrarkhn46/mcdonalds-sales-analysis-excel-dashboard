# mcdonalds-sales-analysis-excel-dashboard
Excel-based sales analysis of McDonald’s data with interactive dashboard, KPI tracking, and business insights.

 Project Overview

This project analyzes McDonald’s sales data to evaluate revenue performance, customer purchasing behavior, and time-based sales trends. An interactive Excel dashboard was built to generate business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

 Business Questions Addressed

What is the total sales revenue by menu category?

How many orders are placed each day?

Which menu item is most frequently ordered?

What is the total revenue generated?

How does revenue vary across months and categories?

What is the average number of items per order?

How do order volumes vary by time of day?

How do sales differ between weekdays and weekends?

What are the top 5 revenue-generating menu items?

Tools Used

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables

Pivot Charts

KPI Cards

Slicers

Interactive Dashboard

 Key Insights

Total Sales: $61,626+

5,370 total orders with an average of 2.28 items per order

Lunch hour (12 PM) recorded the highest order volume

Wednesday showed comparatively lower sales

Burgers contributed significantly to overall revenue

Afternoon time slot generated the highest revenue share

 Business Impact
The analysis helps identify peak demand periods, high-performing products, and opportunities for mid-week promotions and operational optimization.
